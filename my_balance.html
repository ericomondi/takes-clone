Payment process
  • Withdrawal
  • Processing withdrawn funds
  • Funds transfers
  • Payment method changes
  • Payout schedule changes

We transfer payments to our writers twice per month: at the beginning of the month (1st-5th) and in the middle (16th-20th).

Withdrawal

Every 1st and 16th of the month the funds are automatically withdrawn from your balance. It means that there is no separate button for you to press on before every payment period to have your funds withdrawn from the system. This process is automatic and works if your balance meets the requirement for minimum amount.

The minimum amount for your first salary to be written off is $50, for all the forthcoming ones - $100 .

The withdrawal usually takes place in the afternoon (GMT time), the exact time may vary.

Remember, if your balance is written off, it does not mean the funds are already on your account the payment process involves multiple parties and does require time.

If your balance is below $100 on the 1st/16th morning the funds will remain on your account for further accumulation. Hence, if you reached $100 on 16th evening or any other day of the payment period, your funds will be transferred to your account during the next payout correspondingly.

Processing withdrawn funds

After the withdrawal, the funds as well as the required details are forwarded to Financial Department, so they can send the information to intermediaries when needed and initiate transfers.

The transfer period is 4 days long: from the 2nd to the 5th (end of day) and from the 17th to the 20th (end of day) the Financial Department are making transfers to the payment systems.

Note, the transfers take place during the business days only.

Funds transfers

After the funds have been released by our Financial Department to your account, please note that it takes some time for the payment system to process the request.

Transfers via PayPal (powered by Hyperwallet) and WebMoney take up to 1 hour.

For Payoneer Prepaid MasterCard it can be from a couple of hours to 2 business days depending on the loading speed you select. For some countries there is only one option set automatically.

For Global Bank Transfer (powered by Payoneer) it takes from 2 to 5 business days. The transfer time depends on interbank relations working with Payoneer.

Bank Transfer may take up to 5 business days whereas Wire Transfer (both powered by Hyperwallet) up to 7 business days.

Hence, if the payment is sent on the 20th, you may receive it from 20th till 27th depending on your preferred payment method.

Payment method changes

If you want to clarify something about your payment method or change your payment details, you must do that:

  • until the 27th to be included into the payout at the beginning of the month (1st -5th)
  • until the 12th to be included into the middle of the month payout (16th -20th).

Requests received after the mentioned dates might not take place before the closest payout.

If you want to change your payment information, you will have to pass the verification procedure and provide us with the official documents for security purposes.

In order to do that, please send us a message using a Help Desk section of your profile or email us at
manager@writerbay.com.

Help Desk is a recommended way of communication as compared to email (tickets are checked on a priority basis).

Payout schedule change

You have the possibility to select the payout schedule that would be most convenient for you.

We provide four options for you to choose from:

  • At the beginning of the month (every 1st day of the month the funds will be withdrawn and sent to you by 5th, end of the day).
  • In the middle of the month (the withdrawal will take place on the 16th each month and the funds will be sent by 20th, end of the day).
  • Twice per month both in the 1st-5th time frame and 16th-20th.
  • Keep the funds on your balance and accumulate some planned amount, in order to have the funds transferred altogether (and paying transfer fees only once).

Note, if you are switching your payment period after the funds were written off on a withdrawal day (1st, 16th), the changes will be applied to the next payout period (not to the current one).

Same rule applies if you change your mind and select a different schedule while the payout period is still in progress we will use your previous schedule for that payment period and switch to a new one afterwards.

Hence, make sure to apply the changes before the beginning of the payout period.

If you need the attention of our Writer Managers on the matter, please raise a ticket via Help Desk by 12th / 27th (depending on the nearest payout date) keeping in mind that the matter will be addressed during business days.

Global Bank Transfer (powered by Payoneer)

Allows you to receive payments directly to your bank account, the transfers are in your local currency. Transfer fee is set by your bank and starts from $2.99. Visit Payoneer website to learn more.

The transfer takes approximately 2 days after the funds release from our side.

As soon as you select this option, make sure to submit the account holder name and press Click here to register with Payoneer button. After that you will be redirected to Payoneer website to continue the application process as well as provide your bank details where you would like to receive the payment in the future.

It takes at least 2 business days for Payoneer to provide you with a feedback regarding your application status. If it takes more than 5 business days for Payoneer to approve your application, please make sure to contact them as there might be a need in additional verification of your profile.

If you already have an account, click on Already have an account? option so to login to your Payoneer account.

You will be able to recheck from your side that your account is connected properly if you login to your Payoneer account, go to Funding sources and will see WriterBay and your Writer ID reflected there.

Payoneer Prepaid MasterCard

Allows you to receive payment to your Payoneer account from where you may withdraw the funds to your personal Payoneer card.

Transfer fee is 0% but you should cover the card maintenance fee. The funds transfer time varies from a couple of hours to 2 days depending on the loading speed you select (you will be able to select one of the options as soon as you receive an email from Payoneer about an incoming transaction).

Note, not all the countries support this option (e.g. India, Russia). The full list of Payoneer card supported countries can be found here.

As soon as you select this option, make sure to submit the account holder name and press Click here to receive Prepaid MasterCard to order a card. After that you will be redirected to Payoneer website, where you will have to continue the application process.

It takes at least 2 business days for Payoneer to provide you with a feedback regarding your application status. If it takes more than 5 business days for Payoneer to approve your application, please make sure to contact them as there might be a need in additional verification of your profile.

After you have submitted the Payoneer card application and it got approved, a confirmation email with the approximate arrival date will be sent to you. Payoneer cards are shipped via standard international mail, free of charge, the usual shipping time is from 10 to 14 days. If you would like to accelerate the process of shipping and use FedEx or DHL, you should contact Payoneer Support Team.

If you already have an account, click on Already have an account? option so to login to your Payoneer account.

You will be able to recheck from your side that your account is connected properly if you login to your Payoneer account, go to Funding sources and will see WriterBay and your Writer ID reflected there.

WebMoney

Allows you to receive immediate payments in USD to your WMZ purse. The transfer fee is 5%.

Note, this payment method is supported not by all countries, you may check the list as well as more information on WebMoney here.

You may register a WMZ purse here.
